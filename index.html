 Terms of Use

Sigpure specializes in digital sound processing including voice and audio compression, recognition and conversion. We do consulting service or contract job for your voice and audio products in the following three areas.

  • Algorithm Development. Sigpure does research and design for your specific voice and audio products. We develop the technology in floating-point or fixed-point C code. All the C codes are in ANSI C for easily running on your platform.
  • Fixed-point Simulation. We define the fixed-point library based on specific DSP. Then we develop the fixed-point code which matches the floating point algorithm based on strict testing criteria. Sigpure's sound processing tools facilitate this fixed-point simulation and testing. Click on the left for more detail on the basic ideas of fixed-point simulation.
  • DSP implementation. In bit-exactness with the fixed-point simulation, we implement the algorithms in either general DSP like TI's TMS series or specific DSP as required.

Compression enables the bandwidth-limited telecommunication, or saves space for digital media storage. Recognition and synthesis provide interface between human beings and machines. Conversion can find applications in entertainment, security and education. Sigpure develops the most sophisticated technologies of digital voice and audio to migrate to your products.

As a special offer, we share some self-developed sound processing tools with customers. These tools do the visualization or conversion of sound files for analysis, edit, comparison, DSP debugging, and etc. We can customize them into different applications as required. Powered with many different tools, we also do some custom sound edit service for different format conversion, sound effect, and etc.
